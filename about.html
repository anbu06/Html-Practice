Know your audience. Providing the right content to the right person, at the right time requires a very in-depth understanding of your target customer. Who are they? What are their main pain points? What about their interests? Set clear objectives. What are you trying to get out of content marketing? Is it traffic? Conversions? Something else? Be as specific as possible here.
